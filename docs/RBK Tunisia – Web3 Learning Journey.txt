RBK Tunisia – Web3 Learning Journey
Overview
This Web3 Journey is designed as a progressive learning path for RBK Tunisia, structured
into three main sections: Introduction, Coding, and Auditing & Best Practices. Each
section is divided into levels, moving learners from foundational knowledge to advanced,
real-world expertise. The journey is practical, industry-oriented, and aligned with current
Web3 standards.
